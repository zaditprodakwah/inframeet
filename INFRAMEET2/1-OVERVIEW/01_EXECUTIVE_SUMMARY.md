# INFRAMEET - Executive Summary

## Overview & Goal
INFRAMEET is an **Adaptive Trust Infrastructure & Omni-Storefront Directory** that empowers professional service providers, brands, academic profiles, and SaaS directories to prove their credibility transparently and securely.

### Core Value Proposition
- **Proof-Based Credibility**: Move away from black-box directory metrics. Users increase their Trust Score by uploading tangible proofs that are vetted by manual verifiers or automated workflows.
- **Organic Growth Loops**: Standardized, premium-styled widgets embed on partner storefronts, offering performance-based rewards and driving high-intent organic back-traffic.
- **Anti-Abuse Hardening**: High-performance spam heuristics, Cloudflare Turnstile integration, rate limiting, and disposable email checks secure all public touchpoints.

### The Problem
Traditional directories are riddled with fake reviews, pay-to-play listings, and non-transparent credibility scores. This hurts trust between service seekers and providers, driving up acquisition costs and lowering transaction success.

### The Solution
A decentralized, public directory with a highly transparent, log-based reputation engine that calculates trust dynamically while enforcing bank-grade safety via Postgres RLS and transactional server-side logic.
