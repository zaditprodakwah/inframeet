# INFRAMEET - Success Criteria & DoD

## Launch Readiness Thresholds
For INFRAMEET to go live, it must pass a rigorous launch readiness evaluation:
1. **Security**: 100% of standard tables covered by functional Postgres RLS.
2. **Performance**: Initial page loads < 1.5s on mobile networks.
3. **Accuracy**: Zero discrepancies between the sum of `reputation_logs` and the calculated `omni_directory.trust_score`.
