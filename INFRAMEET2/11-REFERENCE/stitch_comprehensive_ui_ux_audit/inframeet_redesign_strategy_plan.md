# INFRAMEET Comprehensive Redesign Plan: "Infrastructural Integrity"

## 1. Vision & Psychology
The redesign pivots INFRAMEET from a "service provider" to an "Integrity Infrastructure." 
- **Brand Personality:** Precise, Secure, Ethical, High-Performance.
- **Psychological Anchors:** Trust through transparency (data viz), Authority through technicality (mono fonts), and Ease of Use through role-based navigation.

---

## 2. Visual Identity & Theme (The "Cyber-Trust" Palette)
- **Theme:** "Deep Space Modernity" (Dark-first, but WCAG-compliant Light mode).
- **Core Palette:** 
    - `Primary`: Cyber Purple (#6366f1) - Innovation and speed.
    - `Secondary`: Trust Blue (#3b82f6) - Reliability and academic rigor.
    - `Surface`: Deep Space Blue (#0b0f10) - Depth and professionalism.
    - `Accents`: Emerald Green (Success/Verified), Ruby (Critical Alerts/Anti-Joki).
- **Surface Treatment:** Heavy use of *Glassmorphism* (blur-backed cards) and *Bento Grids* for modularity.

---

## 3. Typography System
- **Heading & Narrative:** `Plus Jakarta Sans`. Modern, geometric, and friendly for readability.
- **Technical & Data:** `JetBrains Mono` or `Roboto Mono`. Used for statistics, code snippets, SLA metrics, and labels to reinforce the "infrastructure" feel.
- **Scale:** Increased contrast between H1 and body text to improve scanning.

---

## 4. Component Architecture
- **Navbar (Accessibility-First):**
    - Integrated Theme Toggle & Font Resizer.
    - Mega Menu splitting "Enterprise Cloud" and "Academic Integrity" paths.
- **Modul / Cards:**
    - "Bento Box" layout for service features.
    - Animated "Telemetry" cards for live ROI and verification stats.
- **Expert Directory:** 
    - "Proof-of-Authority" badges (cryptographic icons).
    - Advanced filtering with predictive search.
- **The "Formulator" (Kalkulator):**
    - Step-by-step interactive wizard (Framer Motion).
    - Real-time ROI graph updating as inputs change.

---

## 5. Page Layout Strategy
1. **Landing Page:** Narrative-driven, focusing on the "Two Pillars" with high-fidelity visuals.
2. **Pricing Configurator:** A dedicated "Command Center" for project estimation.
3. **Knowledge Hub (Insights):** Modular feed with categories (Tech, AI, Academic).
4. **Expert Portal:** A clean, searchable index of verified professionals.
5. **Transparency Center (About/Legal):** High-density text pages with improved vertical rhythm and clear compliance markers.

---

## 6. CSS & Interaction Design
- **Animations:** Subtle `Framer Motion` entrance effects. Parallax on hero elements.
- **Micro-interactions:** Magnetic button effects and hover-induced depth (z-axis translation).
- **Responsive Logic:** Fluid typography and container-based layouts for seamless transition from Desktop to Mobile.
