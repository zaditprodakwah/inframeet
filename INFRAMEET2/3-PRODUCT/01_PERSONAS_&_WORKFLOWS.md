# INFRAMEET - Personas & Workflows

## Detailed Persona Workflows

### 1. The Directory Owner (Persona A)
- **Goal**: Register their brand/SaaS, build an SEO-optimized profile, upload trust proofs, and capture hot B2B leads.
- **Journey**:
  1. Signs up via email or Google OAuth.
  2. Submits an unverified business listing.
  3. Claims ownership via automated email domain challenge.
  4. Uploads certificate of incorporation (Proof) to boost Trust Score from 20 to 45.
  5. Installs the trust badge widget on their landing page to earn organic back-links.

### 2. The Visitor / B2B Buyer (Persona B)
- **Goal**: Verify a provider's credibility before signing a contract or sending money.
- **Journey**:
  1. Searches the directory for a vetted vendor.
  2. Reviews verified credentials, ratings, and active trust score.
  3. Submits an authenticated business inquiry directly via the storefront.

### 3. The Verifier / Content Moderator (Persona C)
- **Goal**: Approve or reject incoming proofs, claims, and flagged content to protect the system's reputation.
